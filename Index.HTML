SMS Consent Agreement

By opting in, you agree to receive text messages from Ella, our automated assistant. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates may apply.

How to Opt-In

To provide consent and start receiving messages, reply YES to our initial SMS.

How to Opt-Out

If you no longer wish to receive messages, reply STOP at any time. You will receive a confirmation message, and no further texts will be sent.

Help & Support

For assistance, reply HELP, or contact our support team at support@example.com.

Data & Privacy

We securely store consent records and opt-in/opt-out requests. These records are available upon request for compliance verification.

Verification & Compliance

Consent records are maintained in a secure database, and message logs are stored in Twilio. This ensures compliance with CTIA, TCPA, and carrier regulations.
